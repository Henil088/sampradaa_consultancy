What is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing encompasses all online strategies used to connect with current and potential customers — from social media advertising and pay-per-click campaigns to content marketing and email automation. At Sampradaa Consultancy, we build integrated digital marketing campaigns that put your brand in front of the right people at the right time.

Our team of digital marketing experts combines creativity with data analytics to design campaigns that not only grab attention but convert visitors into loyal customers. We manage your entire digital presence so you can focus on running your business.

Our Digital Marketing Services

Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising

Google Ads and Bing Ads campaigns precisely targeted to maximize ROI. We manage bid strategies, ad copy, and landing pages to deliver the lowest cost-per-acquisition.

Social Media Marketing

Targeted ads and organic content on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter to build brand awareness and drive qualified traffic to your website.

Content Marketing

Blog posts, infographics, videos, and whitepapers that educate your audience, build trust, and position your brand as an industry leader.

Influencer & Affiliate Marketing

Leverage trusted voices in your industry to extend your reach and drive authentic engagement through partnerships and affiliate programs.

Video Marketing

Performance Analytics

Real-time dashboards and monthly reports tracking impressions, clicks, conversions, ROAS, and every metric that matters to your campaign success.
