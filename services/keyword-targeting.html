What is Keyword Targeting?

Keyword targeting is the art and science of identifying, prioritizing, and optimizing for the specific search terms and phrases that your ideal customers type into search engines and advertising platforms. Getting keyword targeting right is the difference between wasting your budget on irrelevant traffic and attracting visitors who are ready to buy.

At Sampradaa Consultancy, our keyword targeting approach goes beyond basic research. We analyze search intent, volume, competition, and commercial value to build a keyword strategy that drives qualified traffic and maximizes your marketing ROI — whether through organic search or paid advertising campaigns.

Our Keyword Targeting Services

Keyword Discovery & Mapping

We discover thousands of relevant keywords and map them strategically to specific pages on your website for maximum SEO and content impact.

Search Intent Analysis

Understanding whether a searcher wants to learn, compare, or buy is critical. We categorize keywords by intent to match the right content at every funnel stage.

Long-Tail Keyword Strategy

Long-tail keywords convert 2.5× better than generic terms. We identify high-converting long-tail opportunities that your competitors overlook.

PPC Keyword Optimization

Eliminate wasted ad spend with precision keyword targeting for Google Ads and Meta campaigns — adding negative keywords and bid adjustments to maximize ROAS.

Ongoing Keyword Monitoring

Search trends evolve constantly. We monitor your keyword performance monthly and adjust the strategy to maintain and improve your rankings over time.

Keyword Gap Analysis

Identify keywords your competitors are ranking for that you're missing — and build targeted content and campaigns to close those gaps and capture lost traffic.
